Biography

Musical Versatility
& Artistry

Anthony Simmons is a seasoned musician with 23 years of experience, specializing in violin and guitar. His performances blend technical mastery with a diverse repertoire that spans modern pop, R&B, Americana, and classical masterpieces.

Known for a warm, expressive tone, Anthony provides an elegant musical backdrop for weddings, corporate events, and symphony performances. His collaborative spirit often leads him to perform in duos with fellow artists, creating rich, multi-layered soundscapes across Central Florida and beyond.

Beyond the stage, Anthony’s background in biology and microbiology informs a disciplined, analytical approach to his craft, ensuring every arrangement is polished to perfection.
